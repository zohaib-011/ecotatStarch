Description

We are going to form starch from waste water of potato chips. In our daily kitchen routine, we make chips from potato and discard the wastewater. we tried to use that water for starch extraction. Firstly we soaked potato in cold water to remove excessive surface starch so that chips get crispier. That water contains high starch. French fries industries are wasting large amount of starch water every year. Our purpose to extract that starch from waste water, to reduce import of starch from other countries, increase economy and making it eco-friendly.
